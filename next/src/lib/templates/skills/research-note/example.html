Cross-Department Performance Analysis

Research Note · Q1 2025 · n=4 departments

Abstract

This note examines department-level performance scores and headcounts to identify patterns in engagement and resourcing.

Findings

Engineering is the largest department with 32 employees and maintains a solid performance score of 4.2.

Design, while smaller at 12 employees, achieves the highest score of 4.7, suggesting high efficiency per capita.

Limitations

Data covers a single quarter; longitudinal analysis is needed before making structural decisions.
